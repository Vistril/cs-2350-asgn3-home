You've Been Making Pancakes Wrong

HOME

This pancake recipe is incredibly simple but has one minor difference that will change pancakes for you forever.

You will need:

  • Any pancake mix, homemade or store bought, doesn't matter
  • Butter flavoured syrup
  • Nonstick spray
  • All the butter in your house (or just a knob or 2)

Instructions:

  1. Prep your pancake mix, however you made it, until it's just about ready to cook
  2. Before you throw your batter into a frying pan, take your buttered syrup and squeeze a generous amount into the batter. Whisk well.
  3. Grease your pan, and throw in the pancakes
  4. Fry them up as normal, it's done when a toothpick or fork comes out clean
  5. Serve with butter. Syrup optional.
